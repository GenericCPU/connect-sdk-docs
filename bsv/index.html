Welcome to BSV

BSV is a new form of internet money that can be universally accessed across countries, boarders, and ecosystems.



To learn more about BSV: https://bsvblockchain.org/


The Duro

Duro is a smaller denomination of BSV, equivelant to 500 Satoshi or 0.000005 BSV.



Duro is currently an optional setting for users and can be toggled within the HandCash app.


View Public Profile for more details on this setting.
